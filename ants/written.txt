1.1) The method called simulate in the AntColony class runs the whole game.

1.2) Assault_plan tell the bees where and when to go.

1.3) Within AntColony class, there's a method called configure which takes in a parameter a list of Places and uses that information as a layout for the game. In order to add more Places you would need to pass in a longer list of Places to the  method.

1.4) Armor represents how much damage it can take before bee 'dies'. Once its armor reaches zero it will print 'ran out of armor and expired' and then remove itself from the game.
